HOW IT WORKS

Outlier treats your body like a character sheet. Every measurement and every session counts toward your rank.

01

Measure

Enter your body stats — weight, height, circumferences, body fat. Outlier calculates your real percentile using NHANES population data and a Gaussian model.

02

Train

Log workouts in Quick or Live mode. Choose from 10 presets or build your own. 89 animated exercise guides keep your form sharp.

03

Level Up

Your level reflects where you actually stand — not just how many times you opened the app. Watch your percentile climb as your body changes.

04

Dominate

Track progressive overload, earn achievements, keep your streak alive. The cat starves if you don't train. Don't let the cat starve.

Feature set

WHAT YOU GET

Everything you need to track, analyse, and level up — no subscriptions, no ads, no fluff.

> PERCENTILE

Real Rankings

Gaussian distribution based on NHANES population data. See where you actually stand, not where an algorithm wants you to feel good.

> LIVE MODE

Live Workout

Per-set logging with rest timer, pause/resume, undo sets, extend cooldown, and sound feedback per theme. Runs over navigation — won't reset.

> ANIMATED

89 Exercises

Every exercise has a smooth animation guide. Search by category, add mid-workout, merge whole templates on the fly.

> OVERLOAD

Progressive Overload

Automatic PR detection across your session history. Badges per theme — ↑ PR / > UPGRADED / >> IMPROVED.

> STATS

Body Composition

Navy body fat formula with floor clamp, percentile chart, goal line, history and tooltips. Estimates flagged with ≈ for transparency.

> WIDGET

Home Screen Widget

Level, top percentile and streak at a glance. Updates after every workout finish.

> STREAK

Streak & Achievements

Daily streak, 10 easter eggs, achievement system, quests and a cat that gets hungry when you skip training.

> LOCAL

Privacy First

All data stays on your device by default. Optional Google sign-in for cloud backup — you own your data, always.
