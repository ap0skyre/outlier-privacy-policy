# Privacy Policy — Outlier (rpgfitness)

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the Outlier app ("the App", "we", "our") handles your information. Please read it carefully.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Outlier is an independent fitness tracking application. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: **empyreaninu@gmail.com**

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

While you are not signed in, all fitness data you enter — including body measurements, workout history, training sessions, personal statistics, and app settings — is stored **exclusively on your device** using Android's local storage.

### 2.2 Data Stored in the Cloud (Google Account Sign-In)

The App offers an optional feature to sign in with your **Google Account**. If you choose to sign in, the following data is synced to our secure cloud database (Google Firebase Firestore) so that it is preserved across reinstalls and devices:

- Body measurements (weight, height, body circumferences, body fat percentage)
- Workout history and training sessions
- Personal statistics, scores, levels, and percentile rankings
- App settings and preferences

This data is linked to your Google Account identity (user ID) but we do not store your name or email address in our database.

You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time by contacting us at **empyreaninu@gmail.com**.

### 2.3 Usage Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

The App uses **Firebase Analytics** (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage data, including:

- Which screens and features are used
- App session duration
- Device type and operating system version
- Country/region (not precise location)

This data is aggregated and anonymized. It does not include your fitness measurements or personal statistics. It is used solely to understand how the App is used and to improve it.

You can opt out of analytics data collection in your device settings (Android: **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**).

### 2.4 Notifications

The App may send local push notifications (e.g. workout reminders, streak alerts). These are generated entirely on your device. We do not collect any data about whether you open or interact with notifications.

## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Your name or email address
- Precise location data
- Payment information
- Data from third-party advertising networks

We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.

## 5. Health Data

Body measurements and fitness data are considered **health-related data** under GDPR and similar regulations. By choosing to sign in with Google and enabling cloud sync, you explicitly consent to us processing this data for the purpose of providing the App's core functionality (syncing your fitness profile across devices).

You may withdraw this consent at any time by signing out of your Google Account within the App or by requesting deletion of your data (see Section 6).

---

## 6. Your Rights

### For Users in the European Union (GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right of access** — you can view all your data within the App at any time.
- **Right to rectification** — you can edit your data within the App at any time.
- **Right to erasure** — you can request deletion of all your cloud data by contacting us at empyreaninu@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days.
- **Right to data portability** — contact us to request an export of your data.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — you can sign out of your Google Account in the App at any time. This stops cloud sync. Local data remains on your device.

To exercise any of these rights, contact: **empyreaninu@gmail.com**

### For Users in California (CCPA)

We do not sell your personal information. You have the right to know what data we collect, to request deletion, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. Contact us at **empyreaninu@gmail.com** for any such requests.

---

## 7. Children and Minimum Age

The App is intended for users aged **16 and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you are under 16, please do not use the cloud sync feature.

In the European Union, users under 16 must obtain parental consent before using the App's Google account features.

If you believe a person under 16 has created an account, please contact us at **empyreaninu@gmail.com** and we will delete the account promptly.

---

## 8. Data Security

Your cloud data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, which uses industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted to your account only.

Local data on your device is protected by your device's own security (screen lock, Android encryption). We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

---

## 9. Data Retention

- **Local data** — retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- **Cloud data** — retained as long as you have an account. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 6). We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.
- **Analytics data** — retained by Firebase Analytics for up to 14 months per Google's standard policy.
